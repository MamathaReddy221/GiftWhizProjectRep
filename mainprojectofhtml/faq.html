FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Will you deliver fresh cakes?

Yes, we ensure that all cakes delivered by Winni are fresh and of the highest quality.

How can I trust the quality of cake delivered by Winni?

We have strict quality control measures in place to ensure that only the freshest and finest quality cakes are delivered to our customers.

Does Winni provide midnight delivery as well? Is there any extra charge for it?

Yes, we do offer midnight delivery services. Additional charges may apply for midnight delivery, depending on the location and specific requirements.

What if the recipient is not available when the delivery person reaches there? Will you re-attempt delivery?

If the recipient is not available at the time of delivery, we will attempt to contact them via phone. If re-delivery is required, additional charges may apply.

While placing an order, why do I see delivery time disabled?

The delivery time may be disabled due to various reasons such as delivery slots being fully booked or delivery not available for the selected date/time.

Are there any delivery charges?

Delivery charges may vary depending on the location and delivery options selected. Please check the delivery charges during the checkout process.

Are there any hidden charges (Sales Tax) when I make a purchase on Winni?

No, there are no hidden charges. The price displayed during checkout includes all applicable taxes and charges.

How do I know whether my gifts can be delivered at the address of my choice?

You can check the delivery availability by entering the delivery address during the checkout process. If delivery is available, you will be able to proceed with the order.

Do you take same-day orders as well?

Yes, we do accept same-day orders. However, availability may vary depending on the product and delivery location.

When and how can I cancel my order?

You can cancel your order by contacting our customer support team. Please note that cancellation policies may vary depending on the product and order status.

How long will it take to process my cancellation request?

Cancellation requests are typically processed within 24 hours. However, the processing time may vary depending on the volume of requests received.

Can I order a product that is 'Out of Stock'?

No, products that are out of stock cannot be ordered. However, you may be able to pre-order the product if available.

Is it necessary to have an account to shop on Winni?

No, you can place an order as a guest user. However, creating an account allows you to track your orders and enjoy other benefits.

How do I pay for products on Winni?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and mobile wallets. You can choose your preferred payment option during the checkout process.
