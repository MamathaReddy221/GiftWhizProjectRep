6. What licenses are needed to open a bakery business?

A bakery business also requires five essential licenses - GST registration, FSSAI license, Local Municipal Corporation Health license, Fire license, and Police Eating House license. Without the first three permits, you can't start your bakery outlet in India. You can apply for a police eating house and fire license after running your bakery.
